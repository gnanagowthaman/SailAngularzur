
		Validation for All Management

	1.User Management (create & edit) - validation done

	2.Geography Management (create & edit) - validation done

	3.Country Management (create & edit) - no validation for both create and edit

		(It allows all the special characters while edit and create )

	4.State Management - no validation for create and edit as in country management

	5.Domain Management (create & edit) - validation done

	6.Regulator Management - no validation for create and edit as in country management

	7.Regulation Management (create & edit) - validation done

	8.Document Management (create & edit) - validation done

	9.Sub Document Management (create & edit) - validation done

	Country,State And Regulator Management has No Validation
